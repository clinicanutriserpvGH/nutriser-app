# Wallet Card Design Reference

## Farmacias del Ahorro Style:
- White card with rounded corners, clean look
- "MONEDERO DEL AHORRO" title at top with logo
- Name in uppercase
- QR code or barcode at bottom
- Red accent color (we use gold #C5A55A)
- Clean, professional, physical card look
